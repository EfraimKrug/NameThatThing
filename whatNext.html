Now you should have an email. Just open the email, and click on the link.

When you click on the confirmation link, you will come back to our website. You will be presented with a number of possible names for the dots, and you can vote on the three you like best.

When the contest is over, we will send email to every entry to tell you if you won or lost. If you lost, sorry.

On the other hand, when you win, you will receive money to your paypal account (attached to your email address). We highly recommend using paypal.

If you are the only person who suggested the winning name, you get all the winnings. If you are one of some number of people, we divide the winnings up between all the winners.

And that's all there is!

What To Do

  • Open your email
  • Click on link in the email
  • Vote for 3 entries you like
  • See when your name is chosen!
