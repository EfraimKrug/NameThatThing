First you enter your email and name suggestion. Then we email you a response - so that we can make sure it's a real email address. We will never use or sell your email address ever. Except to mail you the final results (and information on your winnings if appropriate).

When you click on the confirmation link in the email, you will come back to our website. You will be presented with a number of possible names for the dots, and you can vote on the three you like best.

In order complete your vote, you pay $1.00. That payment makes your entry eligible for winning, and completes your vote. If you do not pay the $1.00, your entry will be deleted.

When the contest is over, we will send email to every entry to tell you if you won or lost. If you lost, sorry.

On the other hand, if you won, you will receive money to your paypal account (attached to your email address). We highly recommend using paypal. If you have another way you would prefer to recieve the money, we will try to the best of our ability to accomodate you!

If you are the only person who suggested the winning name, you get all the winnings. If you are one of some number of people, we divide the winnings up between all the winners.

The last thing you should know is that if you want to put in another entry, you can... but that entry will again require a vote and a $1.00 payment.

We will determine the winning entry as soon as we have more that 1,000,000 (a million) people who care about the future of these dots, or December 22, 2019. Whichever comes first.

At that point, we will mail every entry email address a final chance to vote. We will count votes, and contact and pay the winner.

And that's all there is! Good luck!
