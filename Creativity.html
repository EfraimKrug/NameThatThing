About creativity. Do you know what it takes to say something that one other person will appreciate? Imagine, if I could say something that one other person could appreciate.

That person would not only appreciate the words I said, but they would think of me as intelligent. As creative. As Interesting.

This opens so many fasinating possibilities! They might consider me, little old me, born in Nowheresville like a Thorton Wilder Play, to be something special. Oh, for just a few moments... for just a day!

But, what if two people heard my words and appreciated them? If two people heard me, and they thought I was impressive. Or interesting. Or imaginative? Just two people - I might have two friends!

Or better yet (I know, what could possibly be better than two good friends) but what if ten people - ten people thought I was creative. If ten people heard what I said, and agreed that the words I had uttered, designed, created - the words I had voiced were beautiful, poetic, exotic, and creative? If ten people thought that about me, I think we would have a group. We could actually start something.

Now my friends... imagine, close your eyes and imagine, all the people in America using the name you came up with for the three dots! Imagine the thrill, the boost, the, the ... my friends, you would singularly be responsible for 'better communication' across America.

I am sure the magazines would interview you. I am sure television would show you. I am sure radios - gosh - even youtube personalities! My friends... Put in your entry now! As we say "Now is the time!"

And that's all there is! Good luck!
