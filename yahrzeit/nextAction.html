What we will do for you

Shalom Aleichem!

We store your yahrzeit information. Two weeks before each yahrzeit on your list, we send you an email to remind you of your yahrzeit. Then again two days before.

Now that you have entered your name and email address, we are sending you an email with a link. Clicking on the link will confirm that you are indeed connected to your email address.

Once your email address is confirmed, you are welcome to enter yahrzeits you would like to be reminded of.

That's it. Totally free. Best of the best,
