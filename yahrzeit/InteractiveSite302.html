Questions we believe could be asked



Do yahrzeits for today show up anywhere?

Yes. While the individual display is running, a series of plaques are listed along the right side reflecting today's yahrzeits.


Does that work with a touch screen?

Yup.


What is 'interactive' about this?



View mode: If an individual is being displayed and someone clicks on the screen - the person viewing is shown a series of pictures (or documents, or whatever you like).


Edit mode: allows a user to enter a series of screens and update entries, or create new entries.


Shabbos mode: none of the touch functions work at all.
